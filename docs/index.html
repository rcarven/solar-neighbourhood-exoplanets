🔭 Exoplanets in the Solar Neighbourhood

Interactive catalogue and instrumental observability simulations · ELT-PCS

📊 View Project Overview & Quick Statistics

This workstation integrates multi-source astronomical archives to analyze confirmed and candidate exoplanets within 10 parsecs of the Sun. We synchronized data pipelines to evaluate planetary system architectures and simulate instrument observability limits for the future Extremely Large Telescope (ELT-PCS).
